About Recipe Planner

Transforming the way you plan, cook, and enjoy meals with AI-powered personalization and smart meal planning.

Our Mission

Why we created Recipe Planner and what drives us

Making Meal Planning Simple and Enjoyable

Recipe Planner was born from a simple observation: meal planning should be easy, but often isn't. Too many people struggle with decision fatigue, food waste, and dietary challenges when planning their daily meals.

Our mission is to streamline the meal preparation process, reduce food waste, and make cooking more accessible for everyone. We believe that with the right tools, meal planning can transform from a chore into a joyful experience.

By leveraging AI and modern web technologies, we're creating personalized meal solutions that adapt to your specific needs, preferences, and available ingredients.

Key Features

What makes Recipe Planner special

Recipe Discovery

Search through our extensive collection of recipes with advanced filtering by meal type, cuisine, cooking time, and dietary restrictions.

Meal Planning

Visually plan your meals for the week with our intuitive drag-and-drop calendar interface. Assign recipes to specific days and meals.

Shopping Lists

Automatically generate shopping lists based on your meal plans. Identify what ingredients you need to purchase.

Inventory Management

Track ingredients in your pantry and refrigerator. Monitor freshness and get alerts when staples are running low.

Nutritional Tracking

Monitor your nutritional intake with detailed macro and micronutrient breakdowns. Set and track nutritional goals.

AI-Powered Suggestions

Get personalized recipe suggestions based on your preferences, dietary restrictions, and available ingredients.
